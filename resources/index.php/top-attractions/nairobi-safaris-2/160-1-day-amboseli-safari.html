1 Day Amboseli daytrip from Nairobi $ 255/ Daytrip from Nairobi

KENYA DAYTRIPS. DAYTRIP AMBOSELI PRIVATE SAFARI PACKAGE FROM NAIROBI/ 1 day Nairobi-Amboseli-Nairobi

Dec 29 :Pick up from the hotel in the morning at 6;45 and drive to Amboseli national park which is famous for its scenery with a backdrop of snow-capped Mount Kilimanjaro, which dominates the landscape, and open plains. Arriving with a short game drive  have lunch and a short rest. Afternoon game drive and later check out of the park then drive to Nairobi arriving around 5-6pm. Drop off at Airport.

Amboseli National Park is located in Loitoktok District, Rift Valley Province of Kenya. The ecosystem mainly savannah grassland spread across the Kenya-Tanzania border. The park is famous for being the best place in Africa to get close to free-ranging elephants among other wildlife species.It is also a home of the big five. Other attractions of the park include opportunities to meet the Maasai and spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro.
