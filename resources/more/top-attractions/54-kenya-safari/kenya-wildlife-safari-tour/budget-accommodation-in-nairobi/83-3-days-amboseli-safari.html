3 days Amboseli safari Package $ 620
Day1 Nairobi- Amboseli
Depart Nairobi early morning and drive south to Namanga, Kenya’s border with Tanzania, arriving in time for lunch. Afternoon game drives in the park returning to the lodge as the sun set. Dinner and overninght nyati safari camp

Day 2 Amboseli National Park
Early morning game drive around Amboseli national park, winding up at the mt. Kilimanjaro viewing point. Thereafter return to the lodge for late breakfast. Mid morning at leisure enjoying the lodge’s facility, including the swimming pool until lunch. Thereafter, proceed for an afternoon game drive till dusk. Dinner and overnight at nyati Safari Camp

Day 3: Amboseli- Nairobi
Early morning Game viewing returning to the lodge for breakfast. Then set off north to Nairobi arriving early afternoon where you will be dropped off at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your onwards flight arrangements.

End of the Safari.
