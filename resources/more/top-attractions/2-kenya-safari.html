7 DAYS KENYA BUDGET SAFARI  $ 1580, TOUR STARTING FROM NAIROBI ENDING IN MOMBASA

7 days Nairobi/masai mara/ lake nakuru/Amboseli/tsavo/mombasa 

Day 1; Nairobi-masai mara 

Pick up from Nairobi hotel and depart for, famous maasai mara world-wide. On arrival in check in at the camp and have lunch. After lunch, you will have a short rest then proceed for the afternoon game drive around the lake until sunset. Dinner and overnight will be at the Miti mingi eco camp.  
 
Day 2 Masai mara
Early morning game drive then breakfast after breakfast go for more game till lunch time, you will have lunch at have a short rest, later in the afternoon proceed for game drives in search of wild or you can do full day game drive with packed lunch. Dinner and overnight at the miti mingi eco  camp.  
 
Dayt 3; masai mara- Lake nakuru
After breakfast  check out and drive to Nakuru check in at the hotel and have lunch then proceed to afternoon game drives. You will be in your exclusive safari van with your professional tour guide to assist you in taking photos, spotting lots of animals and birds. You will have full board accommodation at resort  citymax hotel
 
Day 4 Lake nakuru-Amboseli
Early morning breakfast leave Nakuru and drive to Amboseli, arrive in time for lunch, have lunch then proceed to afternoon game drives in search of wild animals, across the park which gives the best view of the highest mountain in Africa mt kilimanjaro. Later drive back to the camp for dinner and overnight.  Amboseli Eco Camp/Kimana camp
 
Day 5 Amboseli
After breakfast depart for morning game drive  untill lunch time.After lunch go for afternoon game later drive to the camp for dinner and overnight at Amboeli Eco camp/Kimana Camp
 
Day 6 Amboseli- Tsavo East 
Early morning breakfast and  depart for Tsavo East national pack which is renowned for its large numbers of elephants and the famous man eating lions Game drive en route to the Camp. Check in and have lunch at your lodge. In the afternoon, another game drive at the park later dinner and overnight Red elephant safari Lodge
Day 7 Tsavo east - mombasa 
Early morning game drive, breakfast then check out of the park and  drive  to Mombasa/Diani you arriving around lunch time we drop you to your hotel.

Price based on use private  safari minibus .Each of 02 persons travelling.

Price per person US$  1580

KENYA WILDLIFE SAFARI TOUR

Over decades we boast boast ourselves in providing memorable kenya wildlife safari tour from Nairobi.Nairobi safari to maasai mara is one of booked safari,Maasai Mara being the climalix of all the wildlife,from the big five to the small five.  5days/4nights kenya safari gives sufficient time  clients to see animals from the great rift valley lakes to maasai mara the heart of the jungle. The tour is best for families,honeymooners friends,and we group sharing We can customize the tour to fit your budget.

Day1 : ARR NAIROBI

On arrival at Jomo Kenyatta Airport met and transfer to your Hotel.  Balance day at leisure for optional excursions in Nairobi (at extra cost).

Overnight at NAIROBI  HOTEL (Lunch/dinner excluded)

 

Day 2 :LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK/LAKE NAIVASHA RESERVE

Breakfast and depart for Lake Nakuru National Park to arrive in time for lunch .

In the afternoon, enjoy a game viewing drive in the Park. Later drive to Lake Naivasha for dinner & overnight at LAKE NAIVASHA SOPA.

 

Day 3: LAKE NAKURU - MAASAI MARA NATIONAL RESERVE

After breakfast, depart for the famous Maasai Mara National Reserve to arrive for lunch at the lodge.

In the afternoon, proceed for a game viewing drive.

Dinner & overnight at MARA SOPA LODGE/MARA SENTRIM CAMP/ OSERO LODGE

 

Day 4: MAASAI MARA NATIONAL RESERVE

Full day in the reserve with morning and afternoon game viewing drives (or optional full day in the jungle to see as many animals as possible in the game viewing drives with picnic lunch boxes).All meals & overnight at MARA SOPA LODGE/MARA SENTRIM CAMP/ OSERO LODGE. (Optionals: 1.Hot air balloon safari ending with Champaign breakfast in the bush.  2) Visit to a cultural Maasai Village)

 

Day5: MAASAI MARA/NAIROBI

Breakfast at the Lodge and return drive to Nairobi to arrive in time for lunch at the Nairobi Restaurant. Later transfer to Airport for your departure flight back home.
